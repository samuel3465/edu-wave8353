Our Admission Process

At EduWave International School, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds. Our admission process ensures every child is given fair consideration to become part of our vibrant learning community.

Inquiry & School Visit

Parents are invited to visit our school, meet our staff, and explore our modern learning environment.

Application & Documents

Complete our admission form and submit necessary documents including report cards and birth certificate.

Entrance Assessment

Applicants take a brief academic test to help us understand their strengths and readiness.

Enrollment & Orientation

After successful assessment, admission letters are issued and students are welcomed officially.
