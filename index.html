Sports Activities

At EduWave International School, we believe sports build teamwork, discipline, and a healthy competitive spirit. Our students participate in various sporting activities designed to develop both mind and body.

Football

Football is one of our most loved sports, teaching teamwork, endurance, and leadership. Our teams train regularly and compete in inter-school tournaments.

Basketball

Basketball builds strategy, agility, and coordination. Our school courts are always active with friendly matches that promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Athletics

From sprinting to long-distance running, athletics encourages determination and discipline. Our students take pride in representing the school in regional events.

Volleyball

Volleyball promotes coordination and teamwork. Our volleyball team trains with enthusiasm and participates in both friendly and competitive matches.

Our School Classes

At EduWave International School, our classes are designed to nurture creativity, academic excellence, and character development. Each level provides a supportive environment that helps students grow intellectually and socially.

Nursery Class

Our nursery class focuses on foundational learning through play, storytelling, and interactive activities that make education fun and engaging for young learners.

Primary Class

The primary section builds a strong academic base with creative and hands-on lessons that help students develop confidence, problem-solving, and communication skills.

Junior Secondary

Our junior secondary program emphasizes academic growth, moral discipline, and skill development. Students explore core subjects and start identifying their passions.

Senior Secondary

In our senior classes, students are prepared for higher education and career paths through intensive academic work, leadership training, and mentorship programs.
